About

Find Out More About Us

Thank you for your interest in MX Industrial Corporation. Our company is proud to supply and distribute a wide range of steel products nationwide. MX has earned a reputation for consistent and timely delivery of first quality products as world wide wholesaler and importer of vast a range of steel products.

Our Mission

MX Enterprise Systems (as the mother company of MX Industrial Corporation), established in 1994, is one of the most well established and reputable steel supplier/importer in U.S.A.

MX Industrial Corporation will offer you the best products at competitive prices, and provide you with unmatched customer service.

At MX we are always looking to help our customers grow and achieve something better than what they have now.
